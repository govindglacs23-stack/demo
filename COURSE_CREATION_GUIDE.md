# How to Create a Course with Videos for Students

## Step-by-Step Guide for Instructors

### 1. Create a New Course
- Click **"Create Course"** button in your Instructor Dashboard
- Fill in the course details:
  - **Course Title**: Name of your course
  - **Description**: What students will learn
  - **Category**: Programming, Design, Business, Development, or Other
  - **Level**: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
  - **Price**: Set a price (0 for free)
  - **Duration**: Total hours of the course
  - Click **Create** to save

### 2. Edit the Course and Add Videos
- After creating, click **"Edit"** on the course card
- In the Edit modal, scroll down to see two sections:

#### **Videos Section**
- Add video by filling in:
  - **Video Title**: Name of the video lesson
  - **YouTube URL or Video URL**: Link to the video (supports YouTube or direct video files)
    - YouTube examples: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ`
  - **Description**: What this video covers
  - **Duration (minutes)**: How long the video is
- Click **"Add Video"** button
- Repeat to add more videos

#### **Resources Section**
- Add supplementary materials (PDFs, documents, links):
  - **Resource Title**: Chapter notes, slides, code, etc.
  - **Type**: PDF, DOC, or Other
  - **Resource URL**: Link to download or view
  - **Description**: What this resource is for
- Click **"Add Resource"** button

### 3. Publish Your Course
- Once you've added videos and resources, click **"Edit"** again
- Verify all content is there, then close the modal
- Click the **"Publish"** button on the course card
- Course is now visible to students!

### 4. Students Can Now View Your Course
- Students navigate to **Courses** page
- They'll see your published course
- They click on the course and can:
  - View all your videos
  - Play videos directly (YouTube videos play embedded)
  - Download resources
  - Enroll in the course

## Troubleshooting

### Videos not showing?
✓ Make sure the course is **Published** (green status, not gray "Draft")
✓ Make sure you've **Added at least one video** to the course
✓ YouTube URLs should work. Format: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`

### Students can't see my course?
✓ Check if the course status is **"Published"** (not "Draft")
✓ Go to `/courses` page and verify it's listed there
✓ Make sure you used the Edit button to add videos

### Course shows up but no content?
✓ You need to Edit the course and add videos using the video form
✓ Sample courses (with dummy YouTube URLs) are pre-loaded to show how it should look
